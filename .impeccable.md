## Design Context

### Users
Rolecraft is for an individual job seeker working through a focused, local-first application workflow. They move between collecting candidate context, importing roles, reviewing AI-assisted analysis, generating tailored resumes, and manually validating application prefills before they submit anything themselves. They need to stay oriented across multiple steps without feeling that the product is taking control away from them.

### Brand Personality
Reliable, deliberate, crafted.

The interface should feel calm, competent, and respectful of the user's judgment. It should project trust and clarity more than hype or speed. The product helps the user do serious career work with more structure, better recall, and safer automation boundaries.

### Aesthetic Direction
The visual direction should combine premium SaaS polish with the structural clarity of a workflow tool. Favor clean product surfaces, confident hierarchy, cool neutrals, and restrained amber or green accents over editorial rhythm or startup-dashboard noise. Support both light and dark themes. Avoid purple neon, glowing cyber aesthetics, and anything that feels too retro or nostalgic.

The UI should feel like a modern executive workspace: carefully made, contemporary, and productized without becoming cold. The user should see a strong distinction between system suggestions, workflow state, and human review checkpoints.

### Design Principles
1. Make human judgment visible. The UI should consistently show where the system assists and where the user decides.
2. Build trust through legibility. Strong hierarchy, readable states, and disciplined spacing matter more than decorative effects.
3. Feel premium, not templated. Use precise type, disciplined color relationships, and intentional compositions that avoid generic SaaS patterns.
4. Keep workflow surfaces operational. Dashboards, lists, and detail views should remain scannable and efficient even when visually richer.
5. Use contrast with restraint. Light and dark themes should both feel mature and calm, without neon accents or novelty styling.
